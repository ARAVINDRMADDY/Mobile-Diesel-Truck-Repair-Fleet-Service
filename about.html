About TruckFix

Discover the drive, technicians, and expertise behind our premium mobile diesel repair and fleet maintenance service. We keep commercial assets running flawlessly.

Our Story

How The Repairs Started.

Founded over a decade ago, TruckFix was born from a simple realization: standard repair shops and unexpected breakdown delays were costing fleet owners thousands in lost revenue and delivery penalties.

What started as a single mobile emergency truck has grown into the area's premier heavy-duty fleet asset management and mobile mechanical crew. We bridge the gap between heavy shop capabilities and the unmatched convenience of coming straight to your truck yard or breakdown point.

Today, we have serviced thousands of medium and heavy-duty vehicles, empowering transport managers to operate smoothly while lowering their total cost of ownership.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To keep commercial operations moving efficiently by delivering top-tier mobile diesel mechanics and preventative maintenance solutions directly to clients. We focus on transparent diagnostics and cutting downtime using premium heavy-duty commercial truck parts.

Our Vision

To build the nation's most responsive and trusted mobile diesel mechanical network, fundamentally transforming logistics infrastructure through fast, on-site fleet preservation.
