Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our mobile repair services.

How fast can you arrive for an emergency call?
Our average response time is under 45 minutes within our primary service area. We dispatch the closest available mobile unit immediately upon receiving your call.
Do you carry common parts on your trucks?
Yes, our mobile service trucks are fully stocked with common belts, hoses, filters, brake chambers, air lines, and electrical components for all major commercial truck brands.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, fleet cards (Comchek, EFS, T-Chek), and approved corporate billing accounts (Net 30) for established fleet customers.
Can you perform computer diagnostics on-site?
Absolutely. Each of our trucks is equipped with the latest diagnostic software to read fault codes, force regens, and calibrate modules for Cummins, Detroit, PACCAR, Volvo, and more.
Do you offer preventative maintenance?
Yes, we offer custom PM schedules. We can come to your yard during off-hours or weekends to perform PMs, oil changes, and DOT inspections so your trucks don't lose driving time.
